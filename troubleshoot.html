Problem:

Isn't showing up:

  1. Head over to your device's native settings app.

  2. Go to the "About Phone" section.

  3. Find the listing of "Build Number".

  4. Tap "Build Number" seven times until you see a message.

  5. Confirm and return to the settings screen and go to the "Developer Options".

  6. Scroll down until you find/Or search for "USB Debugging".
